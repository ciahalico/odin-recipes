Tilapia Scampi

Description

With the price of shrimp these days, a recipe where you can enjoy the wonderful flavors of scampi while also not breaking the bank, is fantastic. Let's dive right into the recipe for tilapia scampi!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  • 8 halved garlic cloves
  • 4, 6 ounce tilapia fillets, rinsed and patted dry
  • 1 tbs dried parsley

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) and grease a baking dish
  2. Combine the lemon juice, butter, and garlic into a bowl and microwave in 10 second increments until butter and garlic is softened. This should take about 1 minute total.
  3. Lay the tilapia fillets down on the baking dish and pour the mixture you made over the fillets, making sure they are covered completely and evenly. Sprinkle parsley as desired.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for about 40 minutes total, flipping the fillets every 10 minutes, until the fish flakes.
